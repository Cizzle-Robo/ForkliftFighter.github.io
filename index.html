  • Welcome to Forklift Fencing! The chaotic warehouse arena is open.
  • The Garage: Choose from 5 base Chassis types and 5 devastating Melee weapons (plus secret admin codes).
  • Multiplayer: Added support for Local Player 2, AI Trainees, and 4-Player Online Lobbies.
  • Combat System: Swings, Parries, HP tracking, and custom Special Abilities are fully functional.
