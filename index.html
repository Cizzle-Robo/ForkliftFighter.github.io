Patch Notes

v1.3.1 (Current) - Balance Pass

  • Balance Update: Heavy weapons deal more damage. Tick damage increased for Chainsaw and Blowtorch.
  • Cooldowns: Reduced cooldowns across the board (Dio's Time Stop is now usable more often).
  • Chassis Tweaks: Tank HP buffed. Speedster, Drifter, Gojo, and Sukuna received slight speed/acceleration buffs.

v1.3.0 - Garage & Glory

  • New Chassis: The Drifter added. High speed, terrible traction, great for spin attacks!
  • New Weapons: The Fork-Shield (heavy knockback/defense) and The Blowtorch (cone tick damage) added.
  • Power-Ups: Health Kits, Armor Plating, and Overcharge Batteries now spawn dynamically in the arena.
  • Paint Shop: Added custom color pickers in The Garage (Applies in FFA modes).
  • Horns: Added working horns. Honk away! (Rebind in Settings).
